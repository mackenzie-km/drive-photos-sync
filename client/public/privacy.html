Tag and Sync Privacy Policy

Tag and Sync accesses your Google Drive and Google Photos to sync your photos.

We optionally send thumbnails of your photos to Google Gemini to generate search-friendly descriptions. You can opt out of this at any time from the main page.

We do not sell, share, or store your data beyond what is necessary to perform the sync. OAuth tokens are stored securely to maintain your session.

You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account permissions.
